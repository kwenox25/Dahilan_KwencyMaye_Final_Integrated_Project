About Us

Quatro was a canned, fruit-flavoured carbonated drink produced from 1982 to 1989 in the United Kingdom. From 1983 to 1989 it was commonly available in the UK, though production and sale of the drink ceased there in the mid-1980s. The brand was slowly updating it’s visual, however, they lacked consistency and didn’t have an identity that represented the brand as a whole. They needed a big change. A good splash of fizzy drink with real fruit flavours - Quatro is made for soaking in good vibes all day, without the guilt tomorrow. Quatro keeps things honest with no sugar, carbs or artificial additives in sight.

RECONNECT AND INVIGORATE

Whether dining out, or entertaining friends and family, or simply taking a moment to yourself, Quatro gives you a taste of the fresh fruits, where nature is at its finest, and where you can once again reconnect with everything that matters.
